# Presentation: Agentic AI Vehicle Maintenance System

## Slide 1: Problem Statement & Vision
- **Business Problem**: Unplanned vehicle downtime, poor customer experience, and siloed manufacturing feedback.
- **Solution**: An Agentic AI ecosystem that proactively predicts failures and automates the service lifecycle.
- **Goal**: Improve uptime, personalize engagement, and drive manufacturing quality.

## Slide 2: Continuous Monitoring & Failure Detection
- **Mechanism**: Real-time Telematics stream analysis.
- **Agent Roles**: 
    - *Data Analysis Agent*: Detects sensor anomalies (e.g., Battery overheating).
    - *Diagnosis Agent*: Uses RAG to cross-reference historical maintenance data for predictive accuracy.
- **Outcome**: Early warning signs detected before mechanical failure occurs.

## Slide 3: Persuasive Customer Engagement (SAPI)
- **Primary Interface**: Voice-based virtual agent (SAPI).
- **Secondary Channel**: Mobile app notifications.
- **Workflow**: 
    - Master Agent triggers personalized outreach.
    - SAPI handles natural language negotiation for service booking.
    - Automated scheduling avoids manual coordination.

## Slide 4: Real-time System Orchestration
- **Master Agent**: The "Brain" coordinating multiple specialized workers.
- **Security & Compliance**: UEBA (User and Entity Behaviour Analytics) monitors all agent interactions to prevent unauthorized API calls or workflow deviations.
- **Scalability**: Multi-agent design allows for adding more specialized agents (e.g., Logistics, Parts Inventory).

## Slide 5: Manufacturing Quality Insights (RCA/CAPA)
- **Closing the Loop**: Post-service feedback and RCA results are synthesized.
- **Impact**: Actionable insights fed back to manufacturing teams to reduce recurring defects.
- **Result**: Continuous product improvement based on real-world fleet performance.
